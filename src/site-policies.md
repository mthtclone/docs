## Accessibility Statement

This accessibility statement was generated using the W3C Accessibility Statement Generator.

It is provided to communicate the organization’s commitment to accessibility and to demonstrate alignment with WAI-ARIA standards and best practices.

<div class="quotes">
   This accessibility statement applies to the website of International Leadership Collège. The College is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for all users, including people with disabilities, and aims to provide an inclusive online experience that aligns with the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.2 Level AA standard.

International Leadership Collège takes measures to support accessibility across its digital content. Accessibility is included as part of the institution’s mission and ongoing commitment to inclusive education and communication.

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) define requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities and include three levels of conformance: Level A, Level AA, and Level AAA. International Leadership Collège is fully conformant with WCAG 2.2 Level AA. Fully conformant means that the content fully meets the accessibility standard without any exceptions.

The website is built using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, and incorporates WAI-ARIA where appropriate to enhance accessibility. It is designed to be compatible with modern web browsers and operating systems, including current versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Microsoft Edge on Windows, macOS, Android, and iOS. The site is intended to support common assistive technologies such as screen readers and keyboard navigation, and users are encouraged to keep their browsers and assistive tools up to date for the best experience.

At this time, no specific compatibility limitations are known. However, accessibility may vary depending on the combination of user environment, browser, and assistive technology. If any issues are encountered, users are encouraged to report them so they can be reviewed and addressed where possible.

The accessibility of this website has been assessed through internal self-evaluation by the Information Technology (IT) Department of International Leadership Collège. No formal external accessibility audit reports are currently available.

Feedback on accessibility is welcome. If you experience any barriers or difficulty accessing content, you may contact the IT Department via email at communication.department@ilu.edu.mm or by phone at +959 979 515 199. The College aims to acknowledge and respond to accessibility feedback within a reasonable timeframe.

This accessibility statement was prepared and approved by the Information Technology (IT) Department of International Leadership Collège.
